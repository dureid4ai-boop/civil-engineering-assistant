# SAMPLE — Concrete Volume (Synthetic)

Element: slab
Inputs:
- L = 5.0 m
- W = 4.0 m
- T = 0.15 m
- Waste = 5%

Gross volume:
V_gross = 5.0 × 4.0 × 0.15 = 3.00 m³

Waste multiplier:
M = 1 + (5/100) = 1.05

Net volume:
V_net = 3.00 × 1.05 = 3.15 m³

Result:
Order ≈ 3.15 m³ (rounding per batching policy)
